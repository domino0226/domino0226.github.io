Project information

  • Category: Hackathon
  • Project date: 29 August, 2020
  • Project URL: github.com/dheerajhyper69/newtonhacks2020-enigma

I attended my first hackathon during the summer of my sophmore year of high school. This was the first time programming clicked for me; at least the social aspect of it. I had so much fun writing code out with my teammates, discussing ideas, laughing when the server breaking down, and finally submitting the project. Inspired by our love for cryptography, math, and computer science, my team decided to challenge ourselves and code the Enigma Machine. In no ways was it a winning project. We barely had the encryption working at the end, the visuals were messy, and our pages weren't linked together very cleanly but I was mighty proud of what we could accomplish in only 8 hours. I learned a new language, collaborated on code (which I never done before), and experienced the magic of hackathons.
