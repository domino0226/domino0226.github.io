About

With a passion for biology, astronomy, and engineering, I have both the curiosity and the skill set for STEM world. As an aspiring engineer, I hope to participate in internships, summer programs, and jobs in the technology and science industry. Below are some of my extracurricular activities I participate in.

  • Quizbowl Team: I joined the quiz bowl team to practice memory skills, work as a team, and win High School Quiz Show.
  • Academic Decathlon: Focusing on a broad topic every year, I get to immerse myself in ten different subjects.
  • Other Interests: Watching rocket launches, scuba diving, reading science fiction, writing and playing music, and traveling.
  • Marching Band: Playing piccolo in marching band, I participate in football games, parades, and graduations.
  • Jazz Band: Since 2016, I have been a part of Quincy's jazz band. I play upright bass and we learn everything from Latin to rock.
  • Concert Band: Concert band requires a different mindset than marching band and is the most challenging and rewarding bands.
